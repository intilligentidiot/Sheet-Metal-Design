The Future of Precision Laser Cutting in 2024

Published: Jan 24, 2024 By: Engineering Team

How fiber laser technology is revolutionizing the speed and accuracy of industrial sheet metal components.

Industrial sheet metal fabrication has undergone a massive transformation in recent years, heavily driven by the mass adoption of advanced fiber laser technology.

The Role of Precision in Manufacturing

Operating within strict tolerance limits ensures that all components fit perfectly during assembly. The integration of 5-axis CNC machining with traditional forming methods allows for unprecedented complex geometries. Today’s laser configurations permit incredibly tight nesting routines.

Materials selection remains perfectly critical. Choosing between varieties of aluminum, stainless steel, and specialized alloys dictates the thermal and mechanical parameters of the end product. High-wattage fiber lasers are slicing through thicker gauge metals at exponentially faster velocities than legacy CO2 technology.
